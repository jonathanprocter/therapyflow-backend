Looking at the screenshot, I can see the issue - the styling isn't being applied correctly. The colors are still muted/gray instead of the crisp whites and defined colors we specified. Here's what's wrong:

## Major Issues I See:

1. **Cards are NOT white** - they're still gray/off-white
2. **Sidebar is NOT white** - it's the same gray as the cards
3. **No French Blue icons** - Calendar, Sparkle, Search icons are wrong colors
4. **AI badges are wrong color** - should be French Blue
5. **Everything looks washed out** - poor contrast

## The Problem:
The Tailwind CSS classes are likely overriding the inline styles. You need a more aggressive approach.

## Complete Fix - Add this to your global CSS file:
